Let's Fight Back Together!

We are Virtual, Yet Together

Humanity has experienced the most serious global pandemic in a century while the devastating second wave has broken all previous records. India is facing an acute covid crisis and currently, people are dying due to shortage of oxygen cylinders, hospital beds and plasma donars. The situation in Our Country is a bleak reminder that none of us will be safe until everyone is safe. Hence this is a small initiative by our Team Jugaadu where we will arrange the resources for the Covid Warriors and save as many lives as possible.
